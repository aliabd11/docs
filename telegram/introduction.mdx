These are the commands available in the [okbet Telegram bot](https://t.me/okdotbet_bot). You can also use [agent mode](/agent) by starting any message with "hey ok" to trade in natural language.

All commands work alongside the [web terminal](https://okbet.trade) — your account, positions, and settings sync across both platforms.
