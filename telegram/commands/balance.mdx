This is a way to view your okbet balance. 
Your balance is a composite of the different wallets that make up your okbet account.

In order:
- Kalshi: USDC on Solana
- Polymarket: USDC.e in the ETH wallet created for you
- Limitless: Base USDC in the ETH wallet created for you
- Opinion: USDT on BNB Chain

You can export your wallet and retrieve your balance at any time using /export.
