This is the easiest way to place a bet.
Use /bet with a sentence that describes the kind of bet you'd like to place. Our patented™️ AI will attempt to match your desired bet to an available market.

You can also paste a Polymarket link directly into the chat. The bot will parse the link and instantly pull up the market for one-click betting.
