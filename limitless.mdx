Limitless is a prediction market on Base. Trade Limitless markets directly through okbet on both the web terminal and Telegram bot.

## Getting started

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Telegram Bot">
    1. Go into settings and change your preferred market to "Limitless".

    ![title](/images/limitless_settings.png)

    2. Deposit Base USDC to your account using `/deposit`.

    ![title](/images/deposit_details.png)

    3. Find a market by pasting a Limitless link for one-click betting, or use `/bet` with natural language.

    ![title](/images/finding_limitless_market.png)

    ![title](/images/natural_lang_limitless.png)
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Web Terminal">
    1. Use the platform filter on the Discover page to browse Limitless markets.
    2. Deposit Base USDC through the deposit flow.
    3. Trade directly from the market detail panel.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Managing orders

To view existing limit orders, run `/limits` (Telegram) or check the portfolio page (web). To sell, use `/sell` or the sell button on any position.
