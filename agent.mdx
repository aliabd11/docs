Say "hey ok" to trade in natural language — like "hey Siri" but for prediction markets. Works in both the Telegram bot and the web terminal.

The difference between this and other conversational bots is that your critical data is completely sandboxed from the AI. Agent mode never sees your private keys and doesn't hallucinate. It acts as a knowledge router, guiding you to the right action for your query.

## What you can do

**Trade**
- "hey ok, bet $50 on Trump winning"
- "hey ok, place a limit order on this market at 45 cents"
- "hey ok, sell my position"

**Browse markets**
- "hey ok, show trending markets"
- "hey ok, search for crypto markets"
- "hey ok, what NBA games are on today?"
- "hey ok, what should I bet on?"

**Manage your account**
- "hey ok, what's my balance?"
- "hey ok, show my positions"
- "hey ok, export my keys"
- "hey ok, I want to deposit"

**Copy trading**
- "hey ok, track @username"
- "hey ok, show my tracked wallets"
- "hey ok, enable copy trading"

**Sports**
- NBA, NFL, NHL, MLB, EPL, Champions League, La Liga, UFC, Boxing, F1 — just ask.
