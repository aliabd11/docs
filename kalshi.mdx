Kalshi is a US-regulated exchange for event contracts. okbet connects to Kalshi on Solana through DFlow. Trade Kalshi markets directly through okbet on both the web terminal and Telegram bot.

## KYC requirement

Kalshi requires identity verification before you can trade. Complete KYC from **Settings** in either the web terminal or the Telegram bot.

## Getting started

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Telegram Bot">
    1. Go to **Settings** and complete Kalshi KYC.
    2. Fund your account — Kalshi uses USDC on Solana.
    3. Browse Kalshi markets through `/categories`, `/trending`, or agent mode ("hey ok, show me Kalshi markets").
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Web Terminal">
    1. Go to **Settings** and complete Kalshi KYC.
    2. Use the platform filter on the Discover page to browse Kalshi markets.
    3. Trade directly from the market detail panel.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Trading

Kalshi markets support both market and limit orders. Browse sports, politics, economics and more. Your Kalshi positions appear alongside your other positions in the portfolio view.

Kalshi markets also appear in agent mode — say "hey ok, show me Kalshi markets" to get started.
