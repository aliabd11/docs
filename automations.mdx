Automations let you place bets automatically based on advanced signals. Available on both the web terminal and the Telegram bot.

![title](/images/machine_automations.png)

### Insider detection

The machine automatically detects insider buys and sells. When insider activity is detected, you can configure it to automatically buy or sell positions based on parameters you set — creating a truly agentic trading experience.

### Multivariate limit orders

No longer limited to orders that fill solely on price. Use parameters like liquidity, volume, or a combination to create orders that fill when conditions are right. This is useful for markets that don't yet have sufficient liquidity or where a combination of price and volume signals a profitable opportunity.

![title](/images/tg_triggers.png)

### Notifications

Get notifications based on advanced triggers — insiders buying, selling, or a combination of volume, liquidity and price metrics. Available on both the web terminal and Telegram.

![title](/images/tg_autotypes.png)
