The Machine is okbet's intelligent trading layer. It powers the features that make okbet more than just a betting interface:

- **[Agent Mode](/agent)** — Natural language trading with "hey ok"
- **[Intel](/terminal#intel)** — Whale tracking, unusual bet detection, news feeds, and tweet monitoring
- **[Copy Trading](/copy-trading)** — Wallet tracking with automatic trade mirroring

More capabilities are being added continuously.
