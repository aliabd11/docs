Opinion is a prediction market on BNB Chain. Trade Opinion markets directly through okbet on both the web terminal and Telegram bot.

## Getting started

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Telegram Bot">
    1. Go into settings and change your preferred market to "Opinion".
    2. Deposit USDT on BNB Chain to your account using `/deposit`.
    3. Use `/bet` to find and place bets on Opinion markets.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Web Terminal">
    1. Use the platform filter on the Discover page to browse Opinion markets.
    2. Deposit USDT on BNB Chain through the deposit flow.
    3. Trade directly from the market detail panel.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Trading

Opinion markets support both market and limit orders. Your Opinion positions appear alongside your other positions in the portfolio view.
