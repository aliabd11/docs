## Get started

1. **Web**: Go to [okbet.trade](https://okbet.trade) and log in with your Telegram account.
2. **Telegram**: Open [@okdotbet_bot](https://t.me/okdotbet_bot) and run `/start` to create or import a wallet.
3. **Deposit funds** using `/deposit` (Telegram) or the deposit button (web).
4. **Start trading** — browse trending markets, search for a specific event, or just say "hey ok, what should I bet on?"

Questions? Visit [tryokbet.com](https://tryokbet.com) or join [The Void](https://t.me/theokbetvoid) on Telegram.
