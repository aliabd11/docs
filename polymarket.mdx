Polymarket is a first-class citizen of okbet. It's the default platform — no setup required. When you create an okbet account, you're also creating a Polymarket account. Your okbet wallet trades directly on Polymarket's order book.

## Getting started

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Telegram Bot">
    Polymarket is enabled by default. To place a bet:
    - Use `/bet` with natural language (e.g., `/bet $20 on yes for Trump winning`)
    - Paste any Polymarket link directly into the chat for one-click betting
    - Browse markets with `/trending` or `/categories`
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Web Terminal">
    Polymarket markets appear by default on the Discover page. Filter by platform if you want to see only Polymarket markets. Trade directly from the market detail panel.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Trading

Polymarket supports both market and limit orders. Limit orders support custom expiration (1 hour, 1 day, 1 week, or end of day). View the live order book before placing your trade.

## Funding

Polymarket offers flexible deposit options. Run `/deposit` (Telegram) or click the deposit page (web) to see all available deposit addresses.

## Points

By using okbet, you benefit from exposure to Polymarket's rewards program since your okbet account *is* a Polymarket account.
