Points are earned through trading activity on okbet. The more you use the platform, the more you earn.

## How to earn points

- **Place bets** on any supported platform
- **Invite friends** using your referral code
- **Complete quests** (web terminal) — daily check-ins, volume targets, platform exploration, and more
- **Hold $OK tokens** — your token balance contributes to your points total

The exact formula for earning points is intentionally kept under wraps to preserve the integrity of the program.

## Quests

On the web terminal, quests give you additional ways to earn XP and level up. Categories include daily tasks, volume milestones, platform exploration, social actions, streaks, and special events. Check the Quests tab in your profile to see what's available.

## Levels

Your accumulated points determine your level (1–10). Higher levels unlock better fee tiers and other benefits.

## Cross-platform benefits

By using okbet, you also benefit from exposure to Polymarket's rewards program since your okbet account is a Polymarket account. Same applies to Kalshi if you're enrolled — okbet is an all-in-one account.
