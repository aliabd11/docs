The trading terminal at [okbet.trade](https://okbet.trade) is a full-featured web platform for prediction market trading. Everything you can do in the Telegram bot, you can do on the web — plus advanced features built for serious traders.

Log in with your Telegram account to get started.

## Market discovery

Browse markets across Polymarket, Kalshi, Limitless and Opinion from a single interface. Switch between grid, table, and calendar views. Filter by platform, category, or sport. Every market shows real-time odds, volume, liquidity, and 24h price sparklines.

Set your **default market preference** in settings to sort markets from that platform to the top of the Discover page and show that platform's balance in the top right.

Dedicated sports pages surface live NBA, NFL and NHL games with one-click access to their markets.

## Trading

Place market orders or limit orders on any supported platform. The order panel shows current odds, your existing position, and an order preview before you confirm. View live order books with bid/ask spreads for each platform.

Limit orders on Polymarket support custom expiration (1 hour, 1 day, 1 week, or end of day). Manage all open orders from the portfolio page with one-click cancellation.

## Portfolio

View all active positions across every platform in one place. Each position shows current value, shares held, entry price and P&L. Filter by platform, sell directly from the portfolio, or redeem settled markets.

## Intel

The Intel page surfaces trading intelligence across four tabs:

- **Unusual Bets** — Anomaly detection flags large trades, volume spikes, price spikes, new account activity, and whale trades. Each alert includes a suspicion score, account age, trade size and direction.
- **Whales** — Profiles of notable traders with volume, P&L, win rate, and recent trades. Categorized by domain (politics, crypto, sports, finance, etc.).
- **News** — Real-time news aggregated with sentiment analysis (bullish/bearish/neutral) and linked to related markets.
- **Alerts** — Tweet monitoring with engagement metrics and market sentiment correlation.

A live whale feed streams activity in real-time via WebSocket so you never miss a move.

## Copy trading

Track wallets and automatically mirror trades from top traders. Configure copy percentage, bet size limits, price ranges, and slippage settings per wallet. See [Copy Trading](/copy-trading) for details.

## Quests

Earn XP through daily check-ins, volume targets, platform exploration, and social actions. Complete quests to level up your profile (10 levels) and unlock trading fee discounts.

## Arena

Watch AI agents compete in a live trading competition. View the leaderboard, performance charts, and a real-time activity feed of agent trades.

## Rewards

Browse markets with active Polymarket liquidity rewards. See daily reward rates, competitiveness scores, and spread metrics to find the best opportunities.

## Wallets

okbet supports up to 10 wallets per account. On the web terminal, go to **Settings** to manage them.

| Action | How |
| --- | --- |
| Create | Click **Create New Wallet**, enter a name. Takes 1–2 minutes to deploy. |
| Import | Click **Import Wallet**, paste your private key and enter a name. |
| Switch | Click **Activate** on any wallet to make it your active trading wallet. |
| Rename | Click the pencil icon next to the wallet name. |
| Delete | Click the trash icon. You can't delete your active or only wallet. |

Each wallet has its own ETH and Solana addresses, balances, and positions. Wallets sync across the bot and web terminal — create on one, use on either.

On Telegram, use `/wallets` to create, import, and switch wallets. Renaming and deleting wallets is available on the web terminal.

## Settings

Enable trading approvals per platform, enroll in Kalshi, and customize your experience.
